# Performance Tracker (Analytics) Breakdown

The **Performance Tracker** is the central feedback hub for the student. It transforms raw data (quiz scores, mentor grades, completion status) into actionable insights, helping students understand not just *where* they are, but *how* they are performing.

## 1. What's Inside?

The tracker is divided into four key sections, designed to be read from top to bottom:

### 🔹 1. High-Level Performance Analytics
This section gives an immediate "pulse check" on the student's journey.

*   **Previous Milestone Card**:
    *   **Score**: The grade received on the last completed milestone (e.g., 85/100).
    *   **Sentiment**: The overall tone of the mentor's feedback (Positive, Neutral, Critical).
    *   **Feedback Snippet**: A direct quote from the mentor's review.
    *   **Status**: Marked as "Completed".
*   **Current Focus Card**:
    *   **Active Milestone**: The title of the milestone currently in progress.
    *   **Status**: "In Progress" or "Locked".
    *   **Focus Area**: Specific advice on what to improve right now (e.g., "Improve Code Modularity").

### 🔹 2. Mentor's Guidance (Prep for Next Goal)
A dedicated text area where the mentor provides forward-looking advice. Unlike the backward-looking feedback on a specific milestone, this section is about **strategy**:
*   *What coming up next?*
*   *What resources should I look at?*
*   *General encouragement.*

### 🔹 3. Skills Analysis
This visualizes the student's competence across different domains (e.g., Frontend, Backend, System Design).
*   **Radar Chart (Spider Graph)**: A visual shape that shows if a student is "balanced" or leaning heavily into one skill set.
*   **Detailed Breakdown**: A list of technical skills with progress bars. These scores are calculated dynamically from the **average quiz scores** in relevant roadmap sections.

### 🔹 4. Job Readiness & Growth
*   **Job Readiness Score**: A circular gauge (0-100%) that represents how far along the student is in their entire roadmap. A score of 100% means they have completed all milestones.
*   **Growth Trajectory**: A line graph showing the student's performance trend over time. It helps visualize consistency—are they improving, plateauing, or struggling?

---

## 2. How It Helps the Student

### ✅ Clarity & Direction
Students often feel lost in self-paced learning. The **Current Focus** card and **Mentor's Guidance** eliminate ambiguity by telling them exactly what to work on next.

### ✅ Immediate Feedback Loop
Instead of waiting until the end of a course to know if they passed, students get **real-time grades and sentiment analysis** after every milestone. This allows them to course-correct early.

### ✅ Motivation through Gamification
Seeing the **Job Readiness** percentage tick up and the **Growth Graph** climb provides a sense of progress and accomplishment, keeping engagement high.

### ✅ holistic Skill Development
The **Skills Radar Chart** highlights weak spots. If a student sees their "Communication" score is low compared to "Coding," they know to focus more on their soft skills during the next interview.
